Raj game

This game is a multiplayer game so it needs two players to be involved.

Each player will be given 30 seconds time to score well than the other and the one who scores more will win the game.

Player has to tap on the redlogo and on blue logo and then green logo within 5 seconds if not, player will be given an alert message.

Player has to tap on the logos in the order as shown in the above instruction if not, the player will not be given a score for those unordered taps

Game has to be played by each player in their respective frames which was chosen according to their comfort.

The game will automatically ends after 30 seconds and the score of player will be shown in a new document in their respective frame only.

if the player has touched the red one multiple times then he/she has to tap on the blue one those many times and then on green one those many times to score points but clicking on red one multiple times will give you alret messages because of which time will be wasted.
